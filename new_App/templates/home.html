Vision & Mission

At FGEN (Future Generation), our mission is to educate, engage, and empower the upcoming generation with comprehensive knowledge about the brain, fostering a community of informed and inspired leaders. We strive to spread awareness about the intricacies of neuroscience and its impact on our daily lives, nurturing curiosity and promoting critical thinking. Through education, outreach, and innovative initiatives, FGEN aims to equip future leaders with the understanding and skills necessary to harness the power of the brain, driving progress and positive change in society. Together, we envision a world where knowledge of the brain fuels leadership, empathy, and innovation.

Featured Programs

FGEN holds neuroscience literacy sessions at junior high and elementary schools so that they can be more informed about neuroscience and the wonders of the brain! Please refer to the program page for more info!

Educate

FGEN aims to educate students about the brain, making them well-informed about the basics of the brain.

Engage

FGEN aims to make presentations fun, interactive and engaging for students, making it a memorable experience for all.

Empower

FGEN aims to raise awareness on common neurodegenerative diseases and empower all those who may be dealing with any disorder or disease.
